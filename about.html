About

The UITS Cyber Security Wing is a student-driven community. Our mission is to cultivate practical security skills and ethical values through projects, workshops, and competitions.

Mission & Goals

  • Preparing students to navigate and secure the cyberspace of tomorrow.
  • Build readiness for industry certifications and careers.
  • Run hands-on labs and publish open resources.
  • Represent UITS in inter-university CTFs.

Relationship with Bangladeshi Cyber Community

We operate as part of the broader Bangladeshi cyber community. The community provides collaboration, guidance, and support, while our wing focuses on domain-specific security activities and knowledge-sharing.
